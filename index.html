Story Map project inspired by recent travels to a Carkeek Park, located in North Seattle, Washington.
During my time at the park and visit to the beach I noticed a number of signs warning about bringing pets, specifically dogs, to the beach and into a few trails. However, each time I have visited the park, there are contantly dogs in these 'No Dogs' area. This inspired me to create a story map of all the signs that are posted around the park and to make note of the 'willful ignorance' of Seattle dog owners.
